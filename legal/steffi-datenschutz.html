Privacy Notice

for Events and Sessions by Stefanie Gann

This Privacy Notice informs participants about the processing of personal data in connection with workshops, coaching sessions, and women's circles offered by Stefanie Gann.

1. Data Controller

Stefanie Gann

Email: stefanie.gann@gmx.net

2. Scope of This Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice applies to all coaching sessions, women's circles, and related events organized and conducted by Stefanie Gann. It does not apply to events offered by other facilitators.

3. Categories of Personal Data

The following personal data may be processed:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact and booking details
  • Information voluntarily shared in the context of coaching or group discussions

No medical or health-related data is collected as part of these offerings.

4. Purpose and Legal Basis of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Handling inquiries and bookings
  • Communication with participants
  • Preparation and facilitation of sessions
  • Administrative organization

Legal basis:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) DSGVO (performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures)
  • Art. 6(1)(f) DSGVO (legitimate interest in organizing and conducting offerings)

5. Confidentiality of Shared Content

Personal information and experiences shared voluntarily during sessions are treated confidentially. However, participants are responsible for the information they choose to share in group settings.

6. Data Sharing

Personal data is not shared with third parties.

7. Storage Duration

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above or to comply with statutory retention obligations.

8. Data Subject Rights

Participants have the right to:

  • Access their personal data (Art. 15 DSGVO)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16 DSGVO)
  • Erasure of personal data (Art. 17 DSGVO)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 DSGVO)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 DSGVO)
  • Object to processing (Art. 21 DSGVO)

Requests may be addressed to the contact details listed above.

9. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are taken to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

10. Changes to This Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice may be updated if legal requirements or processing activities change. The current version applies.
