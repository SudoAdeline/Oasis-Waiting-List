Provision of health-related information is voluntary. The purpose of processing such data is to adapt the massage safely and appropriately. Health-related data:

Personal data is not shared with third parties.

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above or to comply with statutory retention obligations.

  • Erasure of personal data (Art. 17 DSGVO)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 DSGVO)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 DSGVO)

Consent may be withdrawn at any time with effect for the future.
