Privacy Notice

for Events and Sessions by Thembelihle Mchunu

This Privacy Notice informs participants about the processing of personal data in connection with workshops, sessions, and events offered by Thembelihle Mchunu.

1. Data Controller

Thembelihle Mchunu
Email:

2. Scope of This Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice applies to all workshops, group sessions, private sessions, and events organized and conducted by Thembelihle Mchunu. It does not apply to events offered by other facilitators, even if such events take place in the same space.

3. Categories of Personal Data

Depending on the type of event, the following personal data may be processed:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Booking and communication details
  • Event-related information provided voluntarily

For private sound bath sessions only, health-related information may be collected as described in Section 6 below.

4. Purpose and Legal Basis of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Handling inquiries and bookings
  • Communication regarding events and sessions
  • Preparation and organization of events
  • Ensuring a safe and appropriate session experience

Legal basis for processing:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) DSGVO (performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures)
  • Art. 6(1)(f) DSGVO (legitimate interest in organizing and conducting events)

5. Booking and Communication

Bookings for events may be made via the website booking form. For private sound bath sessions, communication takes place via email. Health questionnaires are sent and returned by email and may be signed electronically.

Personal data is processed solely by Thembelihle Mchunu and is not shared with third parties.

6. Health-Related Data (Private Sound Bath Sessions Only)

For private sound bath sessions, participants may be asked to provide health-related information via a questionnaire. Provision of this information is voluntary. The purpose of collecting health data is to ensure participant safety and to adapt the session appropriately.

Legal basis: Art. 9(2)(a) DSGVO (explicit consent).

Health-related data:

  • Is accessed only by Thembelihle Mchunu
  • Is stored securely
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is deleted once it is no longer required for the purpose for which it was collected, unless legal retention obligations apply

7. Storage Duration

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above or to comply with statutory retention obligations. Health-related data is deleted after completion of the private session and a reasonable follow-up period, unless longer storage is legally required or explicitly consented to.

8. Data Subject Rights

Participants have the right to:

  • Access their personal data (Art. 15 DSGVO)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16 DSGVO)
  • Erasure of personal data (Art. 17 DSGVO)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 DSGVO)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 DSGVO)
  • Object to processing (Art. 21 DSGVO)

Consent may be withdrawn at any time with effect for the future. Requests may be addressed to the contact details listed above.

9. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are taken to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

10. Changes to This Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice may be updated if legal requirements or processing activities change. The current version applies.
